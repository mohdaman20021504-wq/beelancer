Simple process

From idea to delivered — fast

BeeLancer handles everything between you and the creator.

01
🔍
Browse & discover

Search by discipline, software, style, or budget. Every profile includes a real portfolio so you can judge the work — not just the pitch.

02
💬
Connect & align

Chat directly with your chosen creator, share your brief, and agree on scope before any money changes hands.

03
🔒
Pay securely

Funds are held in escrow until you approve the work. No upfront risk — your payment is only released when you're happy.

04
🚀
Receive & review

Get your files, request revisions if needed, and leave a review. Build a go-to roster of creators you trust.
